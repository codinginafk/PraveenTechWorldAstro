## SEO Intelligence Baseline (Google Core Updates 2026)
*Google has explicitly confirmed our approach.*
* **May 2026 Broad Core Update (May 21 - June 2)**: A hard-hitting 12-day rollout that explicitly rewards brands, official sources, and data-rich destinations. Sites that merely compile or summarize content from elsewhere are heavily penalized. Nearly 80% of top results shifted.
* **March 2026 Core Update**: "Information Gain" is an explicitly named ranking signal. Google directly evaluates whether a page contributes genuinely new information versus rephrasing what already ranks.
* **AI-Specific Markup**: Confirmed directly (not rumor) that `llms.txt`, chunking content for AI, and rewriting content specifically for AI systems are all explicitly called unnecessary. Original evidence and firsthand data are what matters.
* **Search Console AI Visibility**: Rolled out June 3, 2026. Impressions inside AI Overviews and AI Mode are now visible directly and must be monitored.
