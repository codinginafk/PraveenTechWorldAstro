### Why You Can't Skip a Password Manager in 2026

Did you know the average person manages over 100 online accounts? I mean, who can remember a unique, complex password for each one? That's why many of us end up reusing passwords, which is a huge security risk. According to reports from cybersecurity firms, more than 80% of confirmed data breaches in 2024 involved stolen or weak passwords. ([NIST Password Guidelines](https://pages.nist.gov/800-63-3/))

A good password manager takes care of this by generating and storing strong, unique passwords for every site. The beauty of it? You only need to remember one strong master password. But keep in mind, not all managers are created equal. Choosing the right one really depends on whether you're an individual, a family, or a business.
